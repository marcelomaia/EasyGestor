About Stoq

Stoq is configured in demonstration mode which you can use to test the software.

While using the Stoq in demonstration mode, the plugins ECF and TEF are enabled and uses two emulators to give a better idea how Stoq works with real hardware.

After installing and enabling production mode the plugins will be disabled, the limitations and emulators will be removed.
